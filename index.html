Leveraging Public Records Requests for Predict Responsive Records Production

Project with the UCSD Data Science Institute predicting whether a records request will produce documents

  • Utilizes a LSTM Recurrent Neural Network to predict whether a request will be produce records with a precision of 72-74%
  • Scraped 17,000+ records requests submitted to the City of San Diego's online portal between 2015-2019.
