Recent Work

What are injury rates like at Amazon warehouses?

Interactive reporting on the injury rates across more than 150 Amazon fulfillment centers from 2016-19.

  • Our findings refute Amazon's claim that warehouse safety has improved during the latter half of the 2010s
  • A follow-up to Reveal's award winning Behind The Smiles investigation

Where ICE reports COVID-19 cases

Interactive reporting on COVID-19 testing and case growth at Immigration and Customs Enforcement detention facilities.

  • Charts case growth at 91 individual facilities
  • Charts national testing at ICE facilities
  • Data was acquired through a collaboration with the Vera Institute of Justice

Disaster Days: Public School Closure Database

Interactive database examining the total number of instructional time public schools lose each year to natural disasters, unsafe conditions and other emergencies in California

  • California lawmaker introduces legislation to give schools the funding to make up instructional days lost to disasters following publication of series
  • 1st Place for Investigative Reporting in the 2019 California Journalism Awards Digital Contest
  • 30+ news outlets reported our findings and database — including KQED, CBSN, The Mercury News, Weather Channel

UCSD Police Department Incident Log Database

Public database of daily calls for service, arrest, and crime information from the University of California San Diego Police Department

  • Data is scraped using python from the PDF copy of the daily log published by UCPD
  • Each PDF is backed up to DocumentCloud due to the department removing records after 60 days and a history of mishandling log records

Leveraging Public Records Requests for Predict Responsive Records Production

Project with the UCSD Data Science Institute predicting whether a records request will produce documents

  • Utilizes a LSTM Recurrent Neural Network to predict whether a request will be produce records with a precision of 72-74%
  • Scraped 17,000+ records requests submitted to the City of San Diego's online portal between 2015-2019.
